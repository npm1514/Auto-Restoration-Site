About Ben and Pierce Auto

Ben has always had an infatuation with cars. He was raised in a family of stock car drivers in western New York. His late brother Billy raced full time at Stateline Speedway in northern Pennsylvania. His entire family worked as the crew for his brother. Billy couldn't make a race one weekend, and his mother, Val, filled in and finished on the podium.

Ben has been involved with driving and working on cars ever since he was a little boy. He was always present when his dad, Big Ben, was working on his old Ford coupe, a car that can be seen in old pictures as a junker in a waste pile. Ben later took it upon himself to surprise his dad by restoring the entire vehicle, which today is his pride and joy.
